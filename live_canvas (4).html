Research & Recognition

Stacey was hand-selected by Northwestern University’s Early Intervention Research Group (Feinberg School of Medicine) to co-design and validate a federally funded virtual autism diagnostics trial, achieving over 85% diagnostic agreement in a cohort of 600+ toddlers.

  • Achieved 84%–86% diagnostic agreement with expert assessments across 600+ children.
  • Reduced average wait times for evaluation from 12+ months to around 1 month.
  • Expanded early-access services to underserved families in rural areas and Chicago’s South Side.
