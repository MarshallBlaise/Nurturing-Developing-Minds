About Us

Stacey Richardson-Ellis, a licensed developmental therapist (DT), co-led Northwestern’s “Reduce the Wait” autism diagnostics project—achieving 85%+ agreement and cutting wait times from 12+ to 1 month.

With 28 years of experience, a Master’s in Early Childhood Intervention, and certifications in infant mental health and play-based therapy, Stacey brings unparalleled expertise to every child’s journey.
